CONVOCATION 2020

Our certificate and online programs for 2021

For the class of 2020, the COVID-19 pandemic delineated and revamped their last year of college. At the end of March, campus closures capsized students’ college lives, compelling them to wind up their final year virtually. And now, students and their families are dealing with the reality of virtual graduations standing in lieu of the traditional in-person ceremonies.
The ceremony kicked off with the lighting of the lamp by the host, Dr. A.P. Krishna, Registrar BIT Mesra. After several months of contemplation, Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra ultimately organised the 30th convocation ceremony on 12th December, 2020 from 11:00 AM. It was reflective of the ground realities during the coronavirus pandemic as the event was colonised through a digital platform, Youtube Livestream, for awarding degrees to the Doctoral, Post-Graduate, Undergraduate, and Diploma Students graduating this year. Instead of joining a procession in caps and gowns and publicly receiving their degrees, the graduating batch of 2019 logged in to the ceremony from home. The occasion was graced by the presence of Smt. Droupadi Murmu, Hon’ble Governor of Jharkhand and Chancellor of the institute. Among the important dignitaries attending the event were Dr. Vijay Kumar Saraswat, Padma Bhushan recipient, also a member of NITI Aayog, as the Chief Guest for the ceremony, and Shri C.K. Birla, Chairman, Board of Governors, BIT Mesra as the Guest of Honour
