OUR FACILITIES

Library

The BIT Central Library serves as a model of an evolving and dynamic facility in technical learning since 1955. The library, as an established knowledge hub that is conveniently accessible by those associated with the University, has a major contribution in making BIT an internationally top ranked Academic & Research driven institution.

Sports Ground

BIT's campus life includes a prolific range of sports activities that keep students sharp and focussed, instilling in them a sense of discipline, healthy competition, teamwork and true sportsmanship. “Never give up” is a credo that every BIT-ian lives by.

Auditorium

G P Birla Auditorium in Mesra, Ranchi is a top player in the category Auditoriums in the Ranchi. This well-known establishment acts as a one-stop destination servicing customers both local and from other parts of Ranchi.
